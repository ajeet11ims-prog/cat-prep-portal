Common Information

Rahul went to an astrologer to learn what the future held for him. The astrologer explained that Rahul had many choices to make and he could only explain what each choice would lead to. The astrologer predicted that Rahul would receive three envelopes yellow, green and blue on reaching home. He would have to choose one of the three envelopes. The yellow envelope would lead to a choice between two jobs. The green envelope would lead to a choice between two areas of business. The blue envelope would lead to choice between two art forms. Rahul would marry a woman whose name begins with 'P', if he either took a job in production or started a business in readymade garments or he became an actor. Rahul would go to the US if he either had a job in marketing or a business in spare parts manufacture or he became writer. In all the six cases, Rahul would go out of India and continue the same work. All the astrologer predicted came true.
