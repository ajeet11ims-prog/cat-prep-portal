Common Information

8 students A, B, C, D, E, F, G and H are to be seated in 8 chairs kept in a straight line along West-East direction. The chairs are numbered 1 to 8 in order from West to East, with chair 1 being at West-most position and chair 8 being at East-most position. A student sitting in a chair can face in any of the four directions viz. North, South, East and West. There is at least 1 student facing each direction.

Following additional information is given:

  1. Students sitting at both the ends of the row are facing only North or South direction.
  2. Any two students sitting on adjacent chairs are not facing each other.
  3. F, B and D are facing in same direction.
  4. A and F are looking in opposite directions.
  5. A is sitting on chair no.5 and looking in East direction.
  6. Any two students who are sitting on adjacent chairs are not looking in same direction.
