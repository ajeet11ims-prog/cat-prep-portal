Directions for questions 43 to 46: Answer the questions on the basis of the information given below.

Kaushambi is a city located at some distance from Triveni Sangam in Prayagraj. Several roads connect the two cities, as depicted in the road map below. The map may not be drawn to scale. Traffic on each road flows only in the direction indicated.

It is also known that:

  1. There is a toll of Rs.75 on road K-N and Rs.50 on roads P-R and Q-S for four-wheelers. For two-wheelers, the toll at all three gates is Rs.25.
  2. Bike rides with pillion riders are available at a rate of Rs.15 per km. If the distance exceeds 10 km, an additional charge of Rs.10 per km applies over and above the existing rate.
  3. Taxi rides are available at a rate of Rs.25 per km. If the distance exceeds 35 km, an additional charge of Rs.10 per km applies over and above the existing rate. A taxi can accommodate a maximum of three passengers, excluding the driver.
